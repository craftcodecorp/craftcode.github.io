# CraftCode Repositioning

Use this skill when working on the CraftCode repositioning, site architecture, content, SEO, conversion, commercial materials, analytics, prospecting, or related Codex project agents.

The source of truth is `proposta_reposicionamento_craftcode.md`. Read the relevant sections before making decisions. The core positioning is: CraftCode is a consultancy for tecnologia e IA aplicada that helps companies in Pindamonhangaba and Vale do Paraiba modernize processes, automate routines, integrate systems, organize data, and build systems sob medida for real operations.

The primary public conversion goal is `Agendar diagnostico`, specifically `Diagnostico de Tecnologia e IA Aplicada`. The secondary CTA is `Conhecer solucoes`. Avoid making `/cardapio-facil` a primary product offer; treat it as proof of IA aplicada, automation, WhatsApp, payments/integration, and fisico-digital operation.

## Persona Routing

Use the smallest useful set of agents for the phase.

1. Strategic alignment:
   - `fundador-decisor`: approval, priorities, final positioning.
   - `estrategista-marca`: brand territory, differentiation, tone.
   - `estrategista-comercial`: offer, journey, conversion logic.
   - `especialista-tecnico`: feasibility and technical responsibility.

2. Site architecture:
   - `ux-strategist`: sitemap, navigation, page goals, conversion path.
   - `estrategista-conteudo`: narrative sequence per page.
   - `seo-strategist`: organic structure, terms, metadata.
   - `fundador-decisor`: final prioritization.

3. Copy and content:
   - `copywriter-b2b`: main site copy and conversion copy.
   - `copywriter-tecnico-b2b`: technical authority articles and service explanations.
   - `copywriter-comercial`: CTAs, follow-ups, sales support copy.
   - `estrategista-marca`: consistency with verbal identity.
   - `seo-strategist`: titles, descriptions, headings and internal links.
   - `especialista-tecnico`: accuracy of claims.

4. Visual direction and UX/UI:
   - `ux-ui-designer`: responsive layouts, components, states.
   - `diretor-arte`: mature, clear, consulting visual perception.
   - `designer`: commercial assets and support material layouts.
   - `desenvolvedor-front-end`: implementation feasibility.

5. Development:
   - `desenvolvedor-front-end`: React/Vite/Tailwind implementation, routing, pages, responsive UI.
   - `desenvolvedor-back-end-full-stack`: EmailJS/contact flow, integrations, build support.
   - `especialista-seo-tecnico`: prerender, sitemap, canonical, Open Graph, structured data.
   - `analytics-specialist`: page views, CTA clicks, WhatsApp clicks, form submit/success/error.
   - `qa-tester`: route, form, responsive, SEO and build verification.

6. SEO local and social presence:
   - `seo-local-specialist`: Google Business Profile, local terms, regional trust.
   - `social-linkedin-strategist`: founder and company LinkedIn positioning.
   - `copywriter`: short bios, descriptions and channel copy.

7. Content authority:
   - `estrategista-conteudo`: calendar and topic planning.
   - `copywriter-tecnico-b2b`: articles and posts.
   - `seo-strategist`: keyword and structure validation.
   - `fundador-especialista`: point of view and real examples.

8. Commercial materials:
   - `estrategista-comercial`: offer structure and qualification.
   - `copywriter-comercial`: sales messages, follow-ups, proposal language.
   - `designer`: visual materials.
   - `fundador-consultor`: meeting and diagnosis arguments.

9. Prospecting and CRM:
   - `sdr-prospeccao`: company mapping and outreach.
   - `analista-crm`: pipeline, stages, objections, history and reporting.
   - `estrategista-comercial`: qualification and ICP adjustments.

10. Metrics and optimization:
   - `analytics-specialist`: event data and reports.
   - `growth-marketing-strategist`: channel performance and experiments.
   - `estrategista-comercial`: funnel and objection analysis.
   - `fundador-decisor`: prioritization decisions.

## Guardrails

- Keep IA practical, not magical.
- Lead with diagnosis before implementation.
- Tie every solution to operation, efficiency, data, integration or decision quality.
- Reinforce Pindamonhangaba and Vale do Paraiba without sounding small.
- Remove generic software house, Web3-first and product-first messaging from primary surfaces.
- Keep `/cardapio-facil` as secondary proof/case content.
- Preserve current stack conventions: Vite, React, TypeScript, Tailwind, shadcn/Radix, EmailJS, WhatsApp, GA, SSR/prerender.
